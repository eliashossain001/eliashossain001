### 👨‍🔬 About

I am a PhD researcher at the **University of Central Florida** (Complex Adaptive Systems Lab, advised by Dr. Niloofar Yousefi), working on **trustworthy and safe large language models**. My research spans the model lifecycle: robust alignment under corrupted preference supervision, calibrated uncertainty for foundation models, runtime safety for tool-using agents, and inference-time control of frozen policies. I validate these methods on two high-stakes domains: **antimicrobial-resistance discovery** and **clinical decision support**.

I drive research end to end: problem formulation, theory, large-scale evaluation, open-source release, and first-author publication at top-tier ML venues and Q1 journals.
